Oda

Akim Oda is a town in the Eastern Region of Ghana. It is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and as a commercial center for the surrounding communities. The town is located along the Birim River and is famous for cocoa farming, timber, and other agricultural products. Akim Oda serves as the capital of the Birim Central Municipal District and features schools, markets, and healthcare facilities, making it an important hub in the region.
